Anjula Schaub is a Communication Design student at the HTW, Berlin.

She likes to draw pictures, but would like to expand her skills profile and get a job.

In her previous life Anjula was a composer based in the UK.
She still maintains her composer alter-ego, Anjula Semmens, on a part-time basis.

After completing her first degree in Music Anjula absconded from the UK with Mr Schaub.
Between 2010 and 2015 Mr and Mrs Schaub lived in Armenia, Moldova, Belgium and Italy, before arriving in Berlin in the summer of 2015.

Anjula is a Deutschophile.

She is learning to communicate in German and can express herself with some accuracy in a widening range of situations.
